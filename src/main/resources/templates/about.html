Our story and mission

Cook Easy is a web application for sharing a vast range of recipes. Its goal is to preserve traditional and not so traditional recipes and stimulate people's desire to consume more homemade food. It is clear from its name that cooking does not have to be difficult. In addition, the design is specifically made to be accessible, understandable and easy to navigate for the end user. This is why "Cooking with Cook Easy is TaStY, EaSY, IntEReStInG".
